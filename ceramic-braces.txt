Ceramic Braces
   1. Ceramic Braces - Ceramic Braces
Title: Ceramic Braces
Body: 
Ceramic braces work just like traditional braces, but they're made with clear or tooth-colored brackets instead of metal ones. These brackets are molded to the front of your teeth and connected by archwires, which are held in place with small elastic bands. Together, these devices apply gentle pressure to move your teeth into alignment.


Ceramic braces are a popular choice for patients who want effective treatment with a more subtle look. Because the brackets blend in with your natural tooth color, they're far less noticeable than metal braces while still delivering the same reliable results!
